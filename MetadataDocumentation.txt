Data Used in this Package:

    Cavalieri, D. J., C. L. Parkinson, P. Gloersen, and H. J. Zwally. 1996, updated yearly. 
    Sea Ice Concentrations from Nimbus-7 SMMR and DMSP SSM/I-SSMIS Passive Microwave Data, Version 1.
    Antarctica, 1979-2015. Boulder, Colorado USA. NASA National Snow and Ice Data Center Distributed 
    Active Archive Center. doi: http://dx.doi.org/10.5067/8GQ8LZQVL0VL. 11 November 2016.
